Measurement Of Electricity

Electricity is measured in watts.The name watts was discovered by a man named James Watt, and the unit power of watt is equal to one joule of work done per second. While volt is the unit for electrical potential difference, and electromotive force(EMF). Volt = Joules / coulomb. The name volt was given by a Italian man and physicst named Alessandro Volta.
